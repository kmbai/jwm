Company Secretary Services

Company Registration

One of the fundamental services provided by JWM Associates is assisting clients in the process of company registration. They guide businesses through the necessary steps to establish their legal entity, ensuring all required documentation is correctly prepared and submitted to the appropriate authorities. This includes:
•Advising on the appropriate business structure (e.g., private limited company, public limited company, partnership, etc.).
•Preparing and filing the necessary incorporation documents, such as the Memorandum and Articles of Association.
•Facilitating the registration process with the relevant government authorities.

Compliance with Companies Act cap 486

JWM and Associates ensures that all their clients' companies are fully compliant with the Companies Act cap 486. They monitor any changes to the regulatory landscape and keep clients informed about their responsibilities. Key aspects of this service include:
•Maintaining statutory registers: JWM Associates assists in maintaining important company registers, such as the Register of Members, Register of Directors, Register of Charges, etc., as required by law.
•Annual General Meetings (AGMs): They help schedule and organize AGMs, ensuring that all statutory requirements are met, including the proper notice period and agenda.
•Filing annual returns: JWM Associates takes care of the timely filing of annual returns with the appropriate authorities, providing accurate and up-to-date information about the company's financial status and structure.
•Compliance advice: They offer expert advice on various legal and regulatory matters, ensuring that the company operates in full compliance with the Companies Act and related legislation.
•Document preparation: JWM Associates assists in the preparation and submission of necessary documents to regulatory bodies for various company-related activities, such as changes in directors, shareholders, and company addresses.
