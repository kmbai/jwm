Taxation Services

Prepare and Remit Various Taxes:

We offer expert assistance in preparing and remitting a wide range of taxes, ensuring that you meet your tax obligations promptly. Our services include handling the following taxes:
Pay As You Earn (PAYE), Value Added Tax (VAT), Corporation Tax, Individual Income Tax, Withholding Tax, Turnover Tax, Withholding VAT Tax, Withholding Rental Income Tax, Fridge Benefit Tax, Capital Gain Tax, Excise Tax, Advance Tax, Import Duty

Online Preparation and Remittance of Statutory Deductions:

In addition to taxes, we provide online preparation and remittance services for various statutory deductions. Our team will ensure accurate calculations and timely submission of the following deductions:
National Social Security Fund (NSSF), National Health Insurance Fund (NHIF), National Industrial Training Authority (NITA), Standards Levy (KEBS), Catering Levy

I-tax Reconciliations:

We specialize in performing I-tax reconciliations with books of accounts ledgers, ensuring that all tax-related transactions are accurately recorded and reconciled. This process helps identify any discrepancies and ensures the accuracy of your tax filings.

Tax Dispute Resolution:

In the unfortunate event of a tax dispute, our team will act as your advocate and liaison with the Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA). We will diligently follow up on your behalf, providing the necessary documentation and supporting evidence to resolve the dispute in a timely and efficient manner.

Overall Tax Consultancy:

Our taxation services extend beyond compliance. We offer comprehensive tax consultancy to help you optimize your tax position, identify potential tax savings, and mitigate any risks. Our experienced consultants will provide tailored advice and strategies to help you make informed decisions and maximize your tax efficiency.
